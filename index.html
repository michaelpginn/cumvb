Thank you for your interest in supporting the CU Men's Volleyball club team! We compete in the National Collegiate Volleyball Federation, playing high-level volleyball against schools from around the country. This year, we plan to compete in Colorado, Las Vegas, Sacramento, and NCVF Nationals in Kansas City. Last year, we won bronze at Nationals, and we're hoping to do even better this year!

We are 100% self-funded by player dues. Between traveling, practices, and equipment, our year costs are roughly $15,000 (with Nationals alone costing over $5,000). For many of our players, this is a large financial burden, so we are humbly asking for your support.

Any size donation helps! The money goes directly to offset costs for players and makes it possible for us to practice, compete, and represent CU Boulder. At Nationals, we will display the names of all our sponsors on our volleyballs and post about you on Instagram (unless you prefer to remain anonymous).

If you would like to donate, please use the links below. If you are donating via the Club Sports website, please also send an email to michael.ginn@colorado.edu letting us know what name we should display for your sponsorship. If you are donating via Venmo, please include "CUMVB" in your note as well as your desired sponsor name.
